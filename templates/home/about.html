About Cake It Easy

We’re a Dublin-based bakery specialising in made-to-order celebration cakes and cupcakes. Every order is baked fresh, decorated by hand, and available for collection or local delivery.

Our Story

Cake It Easy started with a simple idea: great cake should be straightforward to order and a joy to receive. From classic sponges and drip cakes to custom flavours and dietary-friendly options, we focus on quality ingredients, reliable service, and beautiful presentation.

Our online shop is built for convenience—browse, customise, and checkout securely with Stripe. Need something extra special? Use our Custom Cake form to choose flavours, fillings, colours, and an inscription. If you’re in a hurry, pick from our curated favourites and seasonal designs.

Made to Order

We bake fresh for every order—no freezer cake here. Your cake is crafted for your date and occasion.

Quality Ingredients

From Belgian chocolate to seasonal fruit, we choose ingredients that taste as good as they look.

Friendly Service

Clear lead times, honest guidance, and quick replies—ordering a cake should be easy.
